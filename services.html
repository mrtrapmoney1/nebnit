Keeping Your Home Running Smoothly

From the kitchen to the living room, we're here to fix the tech you rely on every day. As a factory-authorized service center for most major brands, we combine decades of experience with the latest training to get the job done right.

What Can We Help You With?

Home Appliances

Your kitchen and laundry room are the heart of your home. We service refrigerators, dishwashers, ovens, washers, dryers, and more to keep your household on track.

TV & Video

It all started with TVs for us back in '47! We're experts in diagnosing and repairing all types of televisions and video equipment, from the latest smart TVs to classic projectors.

Home & Car Audio

Thanks to our merger with audio specialists, we handle everything from high-fidelity home theater systems to custom car audio installations and repairs.

A Family Tradition of Service

We've been part of the community since 1947, back when televisions were just starting to bring Husker Nation into our living rooms. As a family-owned business, we grew by listening to our neighbors. When you needed audio expertise in the 90s, we merged with the best. When appliances became more complex, we trained our team to handle it all.

Today, we're proud to be your all-in-one, factory-authorized repair partner. Choosing us means you're choosing a team with a proven history, a commitment to ongoing training, and a genuine dedication to keeping your home running right. That's the Metro difference.
