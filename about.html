Our Journey Through the Years

1947 - A Passion for Electronics

Our story begins with a passion for the latest electronics. As TVs became a household staple, our reputation for expert service grew, establishing us as Lincoln's go-to specialists.

1999 - The Shift to Appliances

We saw the future. As microprocessors and complex circuit boards began appearing in refrigerators and washing machines, we knew our electronics expertise was more critical than ever. We expanded into appliance repair, applying our specialist knowledge to the entire home.

2010s to Present - The Partnership Advantage

We solidified our role as industry leaders by forming key partnerships with GE, Samsung, LG, Electrolux, Sony and more. These alliances grant us exclusive access to proprietary service manuals, diagnostic software, and technical databases, ensuring our authorized technicians have the information to fix your products efficiently and correctly—an advantage unavailable to most repair shops.
