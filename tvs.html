The Economics of TV Repair

The decision to repair a TV is a financial one. A cracked screen is almost never worth fixing. However, when an internal electronic component fails, repair is often the most sensible choice, especially for the high-end models we specialize in. Our authorized relationships with leading brands give us the diagnostic tools and genuine parts to do the job right.

Sony: Authorized Service for Premium Displays

As an authorized servicer for Sony, we have the expertise to repair their high-performance televisions. A significant investment in a premium Sony TV shouldn't be discarded due to a common electronic failure.

  • Common Repairs: We frequently replace failed power supply boards and main boards on Sony TVs, restoring them to perfect working order for a fraction of the cost of a new set.

LG & Samsung: Expertise in OLED and QLED Technology

LG and Samsung lead the market with their stunning OLED and QLED technologies. Our strong service relationships with both brands ensure we are trained on their latest advancements. When these premium TVs fail, the cause is often electronic.

  • Backlight Failure: A very common and highly cost-effective repair. If you have sound but no picture, a backlight replacement by our technicians can save your TV.
  • Main Board Issues: From non-working HDMI ports to smart feature failures, a main board replacement is a viable repair that we perform regularly.
