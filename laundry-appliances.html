Your high-efficiency washer and dryer are run by sophisticated electronics. When they fail, you need a technician who understands the technology. Our authorized relationships with the industry's top brands ensure a professional and reliable repair.

Decoding the Brains of Your Laundry Room

The shift from mechanical timers to digital control boards has revolutionized laundry appliances, but it has also created new points of failure. Error codes on a display are the new language of a broken machine, and we are fluent in it. Our deep experience in electronics allows us to trace these codes back to their source, whether it's a faulty main control board, a motor control circuit, or a malfunctioning sensor.

LG & Samsung: Authorized Service for the Innovators

LG and Samsung have driven much of the innovation in the laundry market. As an authorized service partner for both, we are at the forefront of repairing their latest technologies, from smart features to advanced steam cycles. Our direct relationship gives us access to the proprietary diagnostic tools needed to service their complex electronic systems effectively.

Whirlpool: Extensive Experience with an American Icon

Whirlpool remains one of the most common brands in American homes. While the company navigates a competitive market, their products are workhorses that we service extensively. Our technicians have years of hands-on experience with Whirlpool's engineering, allowing us to quickly diagnose and repair their most common electronic failures, which often present as specific error codes (like "F01" or "F07") pointing directly to the circuit board.
