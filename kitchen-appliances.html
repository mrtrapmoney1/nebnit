Our Electronics-First Advantage

Appliances have evolved. A modern refrigerator or dishwasher has more in common with a computer than with its predecessors from 20 years ago. This is where our deep, long-standing relationship with electronics gives us a critical edge. We understand the intricate control boards, sensors, and software that manage your appliances. Our authorized relationships with industry leaders are built on this foundation of electronic expertise.
